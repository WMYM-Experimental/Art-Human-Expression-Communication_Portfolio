Weekly Summary



This week we were able to learn about how religion, culture and art are closely related and how these can be a bridge not only between people but also between cultures.

We can also see art as a tool capable of not only helping human beings but also saving them and getting them out of difficult situations.

In what ways can art become a voice for people and their different needs?

Art, over the centuries, has served as an instrument to express and sublimate the experience of human beings. However, today it takes on a meaning that many ignored, that of channeling pain and all those negative situations in order to heal and create something beautiful from it, this is something that he knows very well.
