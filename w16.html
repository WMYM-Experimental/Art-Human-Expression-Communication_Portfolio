Why should value be given to art in all educational processes?



You must have art as a subject in every educational process since, in my opinion, this helps the student to motivate, encourage and exploit their creativity, not only in the artistic sector but also in whatever the subject to be studied, it would also help the student to have a better perception of the world in which he lives, a broader conception of the world, would help to have a better perception of anything he studies, since everything that is learned must be found a form of creativity. Art is one of the most beautiful things that exist, it is a guide to life,
