Weekly Opinion

What is the value we give to art based on?
I think it is based on the idea that the essence of art is an idea or concept, the perception of beauty or harmony, and that it can exist in different ways and in the absence of an object as its representation.

How has my perception of art changed throughout the semester?
My perception of art has changed a lot since I started the course, at the beginning I only saw art as a representation of extreme aptitude and talent like the works of Velasquez for example, but over time I have realized that art is sometimes even in the simplest forms
